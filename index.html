Welcome to Chilli's page!

You are probably wondering why a random cat has a whole page dedicated to her. Well...the answer is because she deserves it. Chilli is the cutest, friendliest, and most lovable cat ever! She has many hobbies such as playing with strings and lasers, eating, sleeping, and getting pets. She has lived a long and adventurous life moving from Houston to Austin, then to Dallas, and back again to Houston. Who knows where her next adventure will take her. Some of the cats that have shared this journey with her are highlighted below. Take a look!

Chilli has four cat friends!

Fruppy

Fruppy grew up with Chilli. They stayed together up until Chilli moved back to Houston. They were the bestest of friends and always looked out for each other. Unfortunately, Fruppy stayed behind in Dallas. Chilli misses him every day.

Romeo

Romeo is one of the two cats Chilli currently lives with. He gets annoyed of her a lot, sort of like an older sibling. He likes to be outdoors so they don't see each other very often. Sometimes they lounge around in the same room.

Snow

Snow has a very complicated relationship with Chilli. They have to be kept away from each other because they fight a lot. Snow likes to play with lasers and look outside windows. He is the newest cat in Chilli's life.

Tom

Tom spends the most time with Chilli these days. He likes to pretend he's not a real cat, and it gets pretty creepy sometimes. He can hold his breath for extraordinarily long periods of time and doesn't have any particular hobbies.
